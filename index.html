Passive Or Sealed Environment Designss

Passive ventilation greenhouses and sealed environment greenhouses offer two distinct approaches to indoor gardening. Passive ventilation greenhouses rely on natural airflow and temperature regulation to create optimal growing conditions, while sealed environment greenhouses use sophisticated climate control systems to precisely regulate temperature, humidity, and ventilation.

Passive ventilation greenhouses are often preferred by small-scale growers and hobbyists due to their energy efficiency and lower operating costs. However, sealed environment greenhouses may be necessary in certain climates or for specific crops that require precise environmental conditions.
